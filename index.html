    Home

    Giving your home a makeover doesn't have to be expensive. With a few small touches and Walmart's Everyday Low Prices on home furnishings and decor, you can breathe new life into your living space.

    Rethinking your living room? You may want a new sofa, arm chair or accent table. But you can also refresh the look of the room with smaller changes: reframe your pictures, add some wall art or get a new slipcover for the couch. Decorative accent pillows are a great, inexpensive way to give your living room some personality.

    For the bedroom, pick up a new bedding set or throw for your bed, or add decorative pillows. If you have a wall mirror, mount a shelf underneath it and add some candles: the mirror will reflect the flickering light and give the room a romantic ambiance.

    The kitchen can easily be made more inviting by adding a fresh coat of paint to the cabinets and walls. For finishing touches, get some color-coordinated kitchen towels or potholders, and place a new plant by the kitchen window.

    New window treatments and lighting can perk up the atmosphere throughout your home. Choose window furnishings that add color and style and let in natural light. Use accent lights to highlight your favorite keepsakes and add light fixtures that complement your decor.

    For more tips, visit our Home Style and Decor Center.
